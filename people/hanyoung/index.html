  Bio Sketch
Jang is a Ph.D. student at Korea University. He received both of M.S. and B.S. degrees in the College of Information and Communications at Korea University. Since 2005, he has been working in the fields of collision detection and accessibility analysis for robotics. Currently, his primary research interest lies in real-time physics simulation.
