Kiwon Um
 um.kiwon at gmail dot com

Research Professor
Korea University

My research interests focus on physically-based simulations of fluids and deformable bodies in computer graphics. I received my Ph.D. in computer science and engineering from Korea University in February 2014, where my advisor was JungHyun Han. I obtained my M.Sc. degree from the same university in February 2008.

I will join to the Nils' group at TU Munich and start working as a postdoc on July 1st.
